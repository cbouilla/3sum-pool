Joining the 3SUM-pool

ONE MORE TIME : YOU WILL NOT BE PAID ANYTHING FOR PARTICIPATING TO THIS POOL.

This being said, contributing to the collision search is very easy.

1. Configure your device

Your miner has to be pointed to the stratum server below : 
URL: stratum+tcp://3sumpool.fil.cool:9998
userID: userName
password: deviceDescription

You can use any userName you want (for instance: firstName.lastName or email@provider.com or nastyPseudonym or anonymous.coward). There is no authentication mecanism and you don't have to register anywhere. Please keep in mind that your userName will be visible on this website.

Please set deviceDescription (the password) to something that identifies the model of your mining device (e.g. AntMinerS5, Avalon741, GekkoScience, ...).

If you have several mining devices (yay!), you may give them distinct userNames, but you don't have to.

See the Data page.
